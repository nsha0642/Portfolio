# Ask Me AI Agent - Example Questions

Try asking these questions to test the AI agent's capabilities:

## Personal & Contact Information
- "What is your email?"
- "What is your phone number?"
- "Where are you located?"
- "Tell me about yourself"
- "Who are you?"

## Education
- "What is your educational background?"
- "What is your CGPA?"
- "Where did you study?"
- "What degree do you have?"
- "Tell me about your master's degree"
- "What is your GPA?"

## Work Experience
- "Tell me about your experience at Infosys"
- "What did you do on the EY project?"
- "Tell me about the CoWrks project"
- "What is your work experience?"
- "Have you worked with React?"
- "What achievements do you have?"

## Skills & Technologies
- "What skills do you have?"
- "What technologies do you know?"
- "Can you code in JavaScript?"
- "What is your tech stack?"
- "Are you certified?"
- "What certifications do you have?"

## Internships
- "Tell me about your internships"
- "What did you do at BCG?"
- "Tell me about your General Electric internship"
- "Describe your Morris Garage internship"
- "What was your experience in internships?"

## General Queries
- "What are your interests?"
- "What is your professional background?"
- "Tell me about your achievements"
- "What makes you a good developer?"
- "What projects have you worked on?"

## How the AI Agent Works

The AI agent:
1. ✅ Reads your question
2. ✅ Matches keywords to find relevant resume sections
3. ✅ Constructs context from matching sections
4. ✅ Uses GPT-4o-mini to generate conversational responses
5. ✅ Responds in first person as if you're speaking
6. ✅ Only uses information from your resume (no hallucinations)

## Tips for Best Results

1. **Be specific** - "What is your CGPA?" works better than "Tell me about grades"
2. **Use natural language** - The AI understands conversational questions
3. **Ask one thing at a time** - Clearer responses for single-topic questions
4. **Explore different phrasing** - Try variations like "education", "degree", "studies"

Enjoy chatting with your AI assistant! 🤖
